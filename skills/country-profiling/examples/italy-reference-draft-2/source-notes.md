# Source notes

Draft 2 uses an immunization-oriented source set. It is designed to prepare a
future immunization policy comparison while remaining inside the Country
Profiling skill boundary.

## Reviewed or proposed sources

| Source | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Intesa 2 agosto 2023, PNPV 2023-2025 and Calendario nazionale vaccinale, Gazzetta Ufficiale | Official legal/publication source for the national vaccination plan and calendar. Required for later comparison, but this draft does not parse the full attachments. |
| PNPV 2023-2025 page, ISS EpiCentro | Institutional public health explanation of approval, publication, harmonization, equity, and calendar-update framing. |
| Vaccines, AIFA | Establishes vaccine regulation, quality control, authorization, inspection, and pharmacovigilance context. |
| Misure di prevenzione sanitaria - Vaccinazioni, Italian Ministry of Health | Supports entitlement context: mandatory vaccinations for minors and free provision according to calendar and ministerial provisions. |
| Vaccini e vaccinazioni, ISS EpiCentro | Source family for vaccination coverage, safety, target groups, and public communication. |
| ISS influenza and HPV coverage pages | Examples of topic-specific coverage source classes, including regional and cohort dimensions. Should be fully reviewed in a later comparison run. |
| Italy: health system review 2022 and Italy: Country Health Profile 2025 | General health system context needed to interpret immunization governance and regional implementation. |
| Fascicolo sanitario elettronico, Italian Ministry of Health | Digital health context because vaccination information is relevant to health records, registries, and SMART data workflows. |

## Source-use cautions

- The official PNPV and calendar should be downloaded and read directly before
  any comparison.
- ISS pages are useful guides, but exact policy requirements should come from
  official policy/legal documents.
- AIFA supports regulatory and safety context; it should not be used as evidence
  of local supply, coverage, or implementation performance.
- Coverage pages identify useful data sources but do not replace extracting the
  latest Ministry/ISS datasets.
