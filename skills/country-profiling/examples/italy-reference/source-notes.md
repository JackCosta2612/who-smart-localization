## Landing-page and endpoint rules demonstrated

- The OECD 2025 profile is listed as `Reviewed` because the reference uses the
  direct PDF URL.
- The Gazzetta PNPV URL is listed as a `Landing page` and `Candidate source`
  because this reference does not parse the evidence-bearing plan/calendar
  attachments.
- The European Observatory and EU country profile pages remain candidate leads
  unless resolved to direct PDFs or reviewed full-text material.
- A landing page, catalog page, search page, or home page should not support a
  substantive profile claim unless its evidence-bearing material has been
  reviewed.
