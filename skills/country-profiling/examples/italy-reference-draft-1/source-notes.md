# Source notes

Draft 1 uses a broad health-system source set. It is meant to show how an
agent can produce a balanced country profile without over-centering the later
immunization use case.

## Reviewed or proposed sources

| Source | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Italy: Country Health Profile 2025, OECD / European Observatory / European Commission | Best current concise overview for health status, system performance, resilience, accessibility, and pharmaceutical policy context. |
| Italy: health system review 2022, European Observatory on Health Systems and Policies | Detailed system source for SSN organization, regionalization, financing, access, workforce, infrastructure, reform history, and implementation constraints. |
| Italy: health system summary 2022, European Observatory on Health Systems and Policies | Shorter reference for universal coverage, national-regional roles, financing, and reform priorities. |
| Health at a Glance: Europe 2024, OECD / European Commission | Comparative European context, especially ageing and workforce pressure. Useful for framing but not a substitute for Italy-specific evidence. |
| Piano nazionale della prevenzione 2020-2025, Italian Ministry of Health | National prevention framework covering NCDs, infectious diseases, occupational health, environment, climate, injuries, and regional prevention plans. |
| Piano nazionale di prevenzione vaccinale 2023-2025 and Calendario nazionale vaccinale, Gazzetta Ufficiale / ISS / Ministry source family | Identifies immunization as a later policy-comparison topic. In this draft it is a downstream source class, not the main evidence base. |
| Fascicolo sanitario elettronico 2.0 and PNRR Salute digital health pages, Italian Ministry of Health | Supports digital health, health information system, and implementation-readiness context. |
| WHO GHO and Eurostat | Proposed structured data sources for later quantitative extraction. Not treated as reviewed evidence in the draft unless country-filtered rows are retrieved. |

## Source-use cautions

- The 2025 Country Health Profile should be preferred for current indicators,
  but exact figures should be extracted directly during a final run.
- The Observatory 2022 sources remain useful for system structure, but reviewers
  should check whether important reforms after 2022 need additional sources.
- The source set is weak for detailed WASH, environmental exposure, workforce
  distribution, and regional implementation data.
- PNPV is identified for later comparison but not interpreted against WHO
  guidance.
